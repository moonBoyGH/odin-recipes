Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato Sandwich

Description

A Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato (BLT) sandwich is a classic, beloved for its simplicity and vibrant flavors. It's a delightful combination of crispy, smoky bacon, fresh and crisp lettuce, and juicy, ripe tomato slices sandwiched between two slices of toasted bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of bread (sourdough, whole wheat, white, or any preferred type)
  • 1 large leaves of lettuce (iceberg or romaine)
  • 1-2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter (optional, for toasting the bread)

Steps

  1. Cook the Bacon:

    In a skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon until it reaches your desired level of crispiness. This usually takes about 3-5 minutes per side.

    Once done, transfer the cooked bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease.


  2. Prepare the Bread:

    If desired, lightly butter one side of each slice of bread.

    This step is optional and can add a nice crunch when toasting the bread.


  3. Toast the Bread(Optional):

    4. Place the bread slices (buttered side down, if using) on a clean skillet or grill pan over medium heat.

    Toast until they reach your preferred level of golden brown and crispiness.

    This step is entirely optional; some prefer their bread untoasted for a softer texture.


  4. Assemble the BLT:

    Lay the bread slices on a clean surface. If desired, spread a layer of mayonnaise on one or both sides of the bread.

    On one slice of bread, place the lettuce leaves. This creates a bed for the rest of the ingredients and prevents the bread from becoming soggy.

    On top of the lettuce, add the cooked bacon slices. Typically, 2-4 slices are used per sandwich, depending on personal preference.

    Layer the tomato slices on top of the bacon.


  5. Complete the Sandwich:

    Place the other slice of bread (with or without mayo, as per preference) on top of the tomatoes, creating a sandwich.


  6. Slice and Serve:

    For easier handling, you can slice the sandwich diagonally or straight through the middle.

    Your BLT sandwich is ready to be served and enjoyed!
